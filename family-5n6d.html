5 Nights 6 Days Complete Andaman Experience

• 2N Port Blair • 2N Havelock • 1N Neil Island

Port Blair, Havelock, Neil Islands

Package Details

Discover the complete beauty of Andaman Islands with our comprehensive 5 Nights 6 Days tour package. This extended itinerary allows you to explore all major attractions at a relaxed pace, including Port Blair's historical sites, Havelock's pristine beaches, and Neil Island's natural wonders. Perfect for families and couples seeking a balanced mix of adventure, relaxation, and cultural experiences.

Detailed Itinerary: 5 Nights 6 Days

Day 1 - Arrival in Port Blair – City Tour

Your Andaman adventure begins as you arrive at Veer Savarkar International Airport. Our representative will warmly welcome you and transfer you to your hotel. After check-in and refreshment, embark on a comprehensive city tour. Visit the historic Cellular Jail, a symbol of India's struggle for independence. Spend time at Corbyn's Cove Beach, enjoying water sports or simply relaxing by the shore. In the evening, attend the emotional Light & Sound Show at Cellular Jail that brings history to life.

  • Airport pickup with meet & greet
  • Hotel check-in and welcome briefing
  • Visit Cellular Jail National Memorial
  • Corbyn's Cove Beach activities
  • Light & Sound Show at Cellular Jail
  • Overnight stay in Port Blair

Day 2 - Ross Island & North Bay Excursion

After breakfast, take a boat ride to Ross Island, the former administrative headquarters of the British. Explore the ruins of old buildings, churches, and the Commissioner's residence, now overtaken by nature. Continue to North Bay Island for water activities including sea walk, glass bottom boat ride, and snorkeling. Witness the vibrant coral reefs and colorful marine life. Return to Port Blair in the evening.

  • Boat ride to Ross Island
  • Explore British-era ruins and Japanese bunkers
  • Visit North Bay Island
  • Optional sea walk and glass bottom boat
  • Snorkeling at coral reefs
  • Return to Port Blair hotel

Day 3 - Transfer to Havelock - Radhanagar Beach

After breakfast, check out and proceed to the jetty for your ferry to Havelock Island. The scenic 2.5-hour journey offers beautiful views of the Andaman Sea. Upon arrival, check into your beach resort and head to the world-famous Radhanagar Beach (Beach No. 7). Ranked as one of Asia's best beaches, spend your afternoon swimming, sunbathing, or enjoying beach activities. Witness a spectacular sunset before returning to your resort.

  • Morning ferry to Havelock Island
  • Check-in at beach resort
  • Visit Radhanagar Beach
  • Beach activities and swimming
  • Complimentary photoshoot session
  • Sunset viewing
  • Overnight stay in Havelock

Day 4 - Elephant Beach Water Sports

Today is dedicated to adventure! Take a boat ride to Elephant Beach, one of the best spots for water sports in Andaman. Enjoy complimentary snorkeling and explore the vibrant underwater world. Optional activities include sea walk, jet ski, banana boat ride, and parasailing. Spend the entire day at the beach, enjoying various activities and the pristine natural beauty. Return to your resort in the evening.

  • Boat ride to Elephant Beach
  • Complimentary snorkeling with equipment
  • Optional water sports package
  • Beach relaxation and photography
  • Explore coral reefs
  • Return to Havelock resort

Day 5 - Havelock to Neil Island - Sightseeing

After breakfast, check out and take the ferry to Neil Island, known for its peaceful atmosphere and natural beauty. Upon arrival, check into your hotel and begin exploring. Visit Bharatpur Beach, perfect for swimming and snorkeling. Continue to the Natural Bridge (Howrah Bridge), a unique rock formation. End your day at Laxmanpur Beach, famous for its sunset views and white coral sand. Enjoy a leisurely evening at this tranquil island.

  • Morning ferry to Neil Island
  • Check-in at Neil Island hotel
  • Visit Bharatpur Beach
  • Explore Natural Bridge
  • Laxmanpur Beach sunset
  • Overnight stay in Neil Island

Day 6 - Return to Port Blair & Departure

After breakfast, check out and take the morning ferry back to Port Blair. Upon arrival, you have some free time for last-minute shopping at local markets or relaxation. Our representative will transfer you to the airport for your onward journey, carrying wonderful memories of your Andaman vacation.

  • Morning ferry to Port Blair
  • Free time for shopping
  • Visit local markets (if time permits)
  • Airport transfer
  • Departure with beautiful memories

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to visit Andaman for this package?

The best time to visit Andaman is from October to May when the weather is pleasant with calm seas and clear skies. This period is ideal for water activities, beach visits, and island hopping. Avoid monsoon season (June to September) as ferry services may be disrupted.

Is this package suitable for families with children?

Yes, absolutely! This 3N/4D package is perfect for families with children. All activities are family-friendly and safe. The beaches have shallow waters suitable for kids, and our tour coordinators ensure special care for families traveling with children.

Are flight tickets included in the package price?

No, flight tickets to and from Port Blair are not included in the package price. However, we can assist you in booking flights at competitive rates. We recommend booking flights well in advance for better prices and availability.

What type of accommodation is provided in the Standard package?

The Standard package includes comfortable 3-star hotels with clean rooms, modern amenities, and complimentary breakfast. All hotels are centrally located near beaches and major attractions. We can upgrade to Deluxe (4-star) hotels upon request.

Is snorkeling equipment provided, and is it safe for beginners?

Yes, complimentary snorkeling equipment (mask, snorkel, life jacket) is provided at Elephant Beach. Our trained guides ensure complete safety for beginners. Life jackets are mandatory, and shallow water areas are designated for first-timers.

Can I customize this package according to my preferences?

Absolutely! We offer complete customization of itineraries. You can add or remove activities, upgrade hotels, extend your stay, or include additional islands. Contact our team at 8900909900 or 9933202248 to discuss your requirements.

What is the cancellation policy for this package?

Cancellation charges vary based on the time of cancellation: 30+ days before travel - 10% charges, 15-30 days - 25% charges, 7-14 days - 50% charges, less than 7 days - 75% charges. No refund for no-shows. Terms may vary during peak season.

Do I need any special permits to visit Andaman Islands?

Indian citizens do not require any special permits for Port Blair, Havelock, and Neil Island. Foreign nationals need a Restricted Area Permit (RAP) which is issued on arrival at Port Blair airport. All permit charges are included in our package.
