Frequently Asked Questions

Is WiFi really free on Green Ocean-2?

Yes, complimentary high-speed WiFi is available throughout the journey for all passengers. You can stay connected, check emails, or browse social media during your 2-hour voyage.

What makes Green Ocean-2 different from other ferries?

Green Ocean-2 offers premium luxury features including WiFi, fully AC cabin, reclining seats with extra legroom, onboard cafe, charging points at every seat, and HD entertainment systems - making it the most comfortable ferry option.

Can I charge my phone or laptop onboard?

Yes, charging points are available at every seat. You can charge your mobile phones, laptops, tablets, and other electronic devices during the journey.

Is food available on the ferry?

Yes, Green Ocean-2 has an onboard cafe offering a variety of snacks, beverages, tea, coffee, and light meals. You can purchase items during the journey at reasonable prices.

How much luggage am I allowed to carry?

Each passenger is allowed up to 25kg of luggage on Green Ocean-2. This is 5kg more than standard ferries. Excess baggage will be charged separately based on weight.

What is the cancellation and refund policy?

Cancellations made 24+ hours before departure receive 90% refund. 12-24 hours: 50% refund. Less than 12 hours: no refund. Weather-related cancellations by the operator receive 100% refund.

Is this ferry suitable for elderly passengers?

Absolutely! Green Ocean-2 offers priority boarding for senior citizens, comfortable reclining seats, easy access washrooms, and attentive crew assistance. The smooth ride and AC cabin make it ideal for elderly travelers.

Can I book tickets for a group?

Yes, group bookings are available with special discounts for 10+ passengers. Contact us at 8900909900 for group booking assistance and customized packages.
