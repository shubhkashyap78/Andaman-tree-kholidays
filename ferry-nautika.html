Frequently Asked Questions

Why is Nautika more affordable?

Nautika focuses on essential services and efficient operations, passing the savings to customers. You get safe, reliable transport without paying for luxury amenities you might not need.

Is it safe despite being budget-friendly?

Absolutely! Nautika maintains all safety standards with certified crew, regular maintenance, life jackets, and first aid facilities. Budget pricing doesn't mean compromised safety.

Can I book the entire ferry privately?

Yes! Nautika offers private charter options for groups, events, or special occasions. Contact us at 8900909900 for private booking rates and availability.

What amenities are NOT available?

Nautika doesn't have AC, WiFi, entertainment screens, or complimentary meals. However, basic refreshments are available for purchase, and the journey is comfortable.

How much can I save compared to luxury ferries?

You can save 30-40% compared to premium ferries. For a family of 4, this could mean savings of ₹1,500-2,000 per trip!
