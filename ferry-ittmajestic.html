Frequently Asked Questions

Why is ITT Majestic so popular?

ITT Majestic has earned its popularity through consistent quality service, punctuality, comfortable facilities, and excellent customer care. With 4.8/5 star rating from thousands of travelers, it's the most trusted ferry in Andaman.

How early should I book tickets?

Due to high demand, we recommend booking ITT Majestic tickets at least 7-10 days in advance, especially during peak season (October to March). Early booking ensures confirmed seats.

What makes it different from other ferries?

ITT Majestic offers the perfect balance of comfort, reliability, and value. You get AC cabin, refreshments, entertainment, professional service, and proven track record - all at competitive pricing.

Is it suitable for elderly passengers and children?

Absolutely! ITT Majestic is highly recommended for families with elderly members and children. The smooth ride, comfortable seating, clean facilities, and attentive crew make it ideal for all age groups.

What is the ticket price?

Tickets start from ₹1,600 per person. Considering the quality of service, amenities, and reliability, it offers excellent value for money. Group discounts available.

Can I get a refund if I cancel?

Yes, cancellations made 24+ hours before departure receive 85% refund. 12-24 hours: 50% refund. Less than 12 hours: 25% refund. Weather cancellations by operator: 100% refund.
