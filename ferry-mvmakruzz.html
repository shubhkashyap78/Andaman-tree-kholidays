Frequently Asked Questions

What makes MV Makruzz eco-friendly?

MV Makruzz uses low-emission engines, solar power, waste recycling, biodegradable products, and follows strict marine life protection protocols to minimize environmental impact.

Is it suitable for large groups?

Yes! MV Makruzz is perfect for groups with spacious seating and special group discounts. Contact us for group bookings of 15+ passengers.

Are there any plastic items onboard?

No, MV Makruzz is completely plastic-free. We use biodegradable and reusable materials for all onboard services.

What is the ticket price?

Tickets start from ₹1,400 per person. Group discounts available for 15+ passengers. Prices may vary during peak season.

How can I contribute to eco-friendly travel?

Avoid single-use plastics, carry reusable water bottles, dispose waste properly, and respect marine life. Every small action helps protect our beautiful islands.
