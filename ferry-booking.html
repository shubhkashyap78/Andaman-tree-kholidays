FAQs About Andaman Ferry Booking

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Our customer support team is always ready to help you.

How do I book a ferry ticket?

Booking a ferry ticket is simple. Visit our booking page, select your route, choose your preferred date and time, and complete the payment process. You'll receive a confirmation email with your ticket details. You can also call us at 8900909900 or WhatsApp us for assistance.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 24+ hours before departure receive 85% refund. Cancellations made 12-24 hours before departure receive 50% refund. Cancellations made less than 12 hours before departure receive 25% refund. Weather-related cancellations by the operator receive 100% refund. Contact us at 8900909900 for cancellation assistance.

Are the ferries safe?

Yes, all our ferry partners maintain the highest safety standards. All vessels are regularly inspected, equipped with life jackets for every passenger, first aid kits, and operated by experienced crew members. We only work with licensed and certified ferry operators who follow strict safety protocols.

Are the ferries accessible for people with disabilities?

Most modern ferries have wheelchair-accessible facilities including ramps, accessible washrooms, and designated seating areas. However, accessibility varies by vessel. Please inform us about your specific requirements when booking, and we'll ensure you're booked on a suitable ferry. Contact us at 8900909900 for detailed accessibility information.

What is the baggage allowance?

Each passenger is allowed up to 20kg of luggage. Excess baggage will be charged separately at approximately ₹50-100 per kg depending on the ferry operator. Hand luggage should be kept with you, while check-in luggage will be stored in the designated luggage area. Fragile items should be carried as hand luggage.

What documents do I need for ferry travel?

All passengers must carry valid government-issued photo ID proof (Aadhaar Card, Passport, Driving License, or Voter ID). Children should carry school ID or birth certificate. Your booking confirmation (printed or digital) is also required. Foreign nationals must carry their passport and valid visa.

Can I reschedule my ferry booking?

Yes, rescheduling is possible subject to seat availability and may incur additional charges. Rescheduling requests made 24+ hours before departure are usually accommodated with minimal charges. Contact our customer support at 8900909900 or email us at info@andamanbliss.com for rescheduling assistance.

What happens if the ferry is cancelled due to bad weather?

In case of weather-related cancellations by the ferry operator, you will receive a 100% refund or can reschedule to another date at no extra cost. We will notify you immediately via SMS and email if your ferry is cancelled. Our team will assist you in making alternative arrangements.
