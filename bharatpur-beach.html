Bharatpur Beach

Shallow Waters Perfect for Swimming & Water Sports

Neil Island, Andaman

About Bharatpur Beach

Bharatpur Beach is one of the most popular beaches on Neil Island, known for its shallow, crystal-clear waters and vibrant coral reefs. Located just 500 meters from Neil Island jetty, this beach is easily accessible and perfect for families with children. The beach offers a wide range of water activities including glass bottom boat rides, snorkeling, jet skiing, and banana boat rides. The shallow waters extend far into the sea, making it safe for swimming and ideal for non-swimmers. The beach is well-maintained with clean facilities, food stalls, and changing rooms. The coral reefs near the shore are home to colorful fish and marine life, making it a great spot for snorkeling even for beginners.

Water Activities Available

Glass Bottom Boat Ride

View the beautiful coral reefs and marine life without getting wet. Perfect for all ages including elderly and kids. 30-minute ride covering the best coral spots.

Price: ₹500-700 per person

Snorkeling

Explore vibrant coral reefs in shallow waters. Equipment and life jackets provided. Ideal for beginners and families. Instructors available for guidance.

Price: ₹500-800 per person

Jet Ski & Banana Ride

Thrilling water sports including jet skiing, banana boat rides, and speed boat rides. Safe and supervised by trained operators.

Price: ₹500-1,500 per activity

Best Time to Visit

October to May

The best time to visit Bharatpur Beach is between October and May when the sea is calm and water visibility is excellent. Early morning (7:00 AM - 10:00 AM) offers the best visibility for snorkeling and glass bottom boat rides. The beach is less crowded during weekdays.

Best for Activities: 7:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Avoid: Monsoon season (June-September) due to rough seas

How to Reach Bharatpur Beach

From Neil Island Jetty: 500 meters (5-10 minutes walk)

From Port Blair: Take ferry to Neil Island (1 hour), then walk or take auto to beach

Transport Options:

  • Walking: 5-10 minutes from jetty (free)
  • Auto-rickshaw: ₹50-100
  • Bicycle rental: ₹100-200 per day

Entry Fee: Free for all visitors

Important Tips

  • Visit early morning for best water visibility
  • Carry sunscreen, hat, and sunglasses
  • Wear water shoes for coral protection
  • Book glass bottom boat in advance during peak season

  • Carry cash as card facilities limited

  • Check weather conditions before water activities

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Bharatpur Beach safe for kids and non-swimmers?

Yes! Bharatpur Beach is one of the safest beaches in Andaman. The water is shallow and extends far into the sea, making it perfect for kids and non-swimmers. Life jackets are available, and the beach is supervised.

What is the cost of glass bottom boat ride?

Glass bottom boat rides cost ₹500-700 per person for a 30-minute ride. The boat takes you over the coral reefs where you can see colorful fish and marine life through the glass bottom. Advance booking recommended during peak season.

Can I do snorkeling at Bharatpur Beach?

Yes! Bharatpur Beach is excellent for snorkeling, especially for beginners. The shallow clear waters and vibrant coral reefs make it ideal. Equipment and life jackets are provided. Cost: ₹500-800 per person.

Are there food and facilities at the beach?

Yes, Bharatpur Beach has good facilities including food stalls selling snacks, fresh coconut water, and local food. Clean changing rooms and washrooms are available. Shaded sitting areas under trees provide comfort.

How far is Bharatpur Beach from Neil Island jetty?

Bharatpur Beach is just 500 meters from Neil Island jetty - a 5-10 minute walk. You can also take an auto-rickshaw for ₹50-100 or rent a bicycle. It's the closest beach to the jetty, making it very accessible.

What is the best time to visit Bharatpur Beach?

Visit between October and May for the best experience. Early morning (7:00 AM - 10:00 AM) offers excellent water visibility for snorkeling and glass bottom boat rides. Avoid monsoon season (June-September) due to rough seas.

Can I see coral reefs at Bharatpur Beach?

Yes! Bharatpur Beach has beautiful coral reefs close to the shore. You can see them through glass bottom boat rides or while snorkeling. The shallow clear waters make it easy to spot colorful corals and fish even from the surface.

How much time should I spend at Bharatpur Beach?

Plan for 2-3 hours to enjoy swimming, snorkeling, and glass bottom boat ride. If you want to try multiple water sports, allocate 3-4 hours. The beach is perfect for a relaxed morning or afternoon.
