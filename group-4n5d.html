4 Nights 5 Days Premium Tour Package

• 2N Port Blair • 1N Havelock • 1N Neil Island

Port Blair, Havelock, Neil Islands

Package Details

Experience the perfect blend of adventure and relaxation with our 4 Nights 5 Days Andaman tour package. This comprehensive itinerary covers the three most beautiful islands - Port Blair, Havelock, and Neil Island. Discover pristine beaches, historic landmarks, and vibrant marine life while enjoying comfortable accommodations and seamless transfers throughout your journey.

Detailed Itinerary: 4 Nights 5 Days

Day 1 - Arrival in Port Blair – City Tour & Light & Sound Show

Welcome to the Andaman Islands! Upon arrival at Veer Savarkar International Airport, our representative will greet you and transfer you to your hotel. After check-in and refreshment, embark on a city tour visiting the historic Cellular Jail, a poignant reminder of India's freedom struggle. Relax at Corbyn's Cove Beach and witness the mesmerizing Light & Sound Show at Cellular Jail in the evening.

  • Airport pickup and hotel check-in
  • Visit historic Cellular Jail National Memorial
  • Relax at Corbyn's Cove Beach
  • Evening Light & Sound Show at Cellular Jail
  • Overnight stay in Port Blair

Day 2 - Port Blair to Havelock Island - Radhanagar Beach

After breakfast, proceed to the jetty for your ferry to Havelock Island (approximately 2.5 hours). Upon arrival, check into your hotel and head straight to the world-renowned Radhanagar Beach, consistently ranked among Asia's best beaches. Spend your afternoon swimming in crystal-clear waters, sunbathing on pristine white sand, or simply relaxing under the shade of palm trees. Enjoy a spectacular sunset before returning to your hotel.

  • Morning ferry to Havelock Island
  • Check-in at Havelock hotel
  • Visit Radhanagar Beach (Beach No. 7)
  • Complimentary photoshoot session
  • Witness stunning sunset views
  • Overnight stay in Havelock

Day 3 - Elephant Beach Excursion & Transfer to Neil Island

Start your day with an exciting boat ride to Elephant Beach, famous for its vibrant coral reefs and diverse marine life. Enjoy complimentary snorkeling and explore the underwater world. Optional water sports activities like sea walk, jet ski, and banana boat rides are available. After lunch, take the ferry to Neil Island, known for its laid-back atmosphere and natural beauty. Check into your hotel and visit Bharatpur Beach for a relaxing evening.

  • Morning boat ride to Elephant Beach
  • Complimentary snorkeling with equipment
  • Optional water sports activities
  • Afternoon ferry to Neil Island
  • Visit Bharatpur Beach
  • Overnight stay in Neil Island

Day 4 - Neil Island Sightseeing & Return to Port Blair

After breakfast, explore the natural wonders of Neil Island. Visit the Natural Bridge (Howrah Bridge), a unique rock formation created by nature. Continue to Laxmanpur Beach, known for its stunning sunset views and white coral sand. After sightseeing, take the afternoon ferry back to Port Blair. Check into your hotel and enjoy a free evening for shopping or leisure.

  • Visit Natural Bridge (Howrah Bridge)
  • Explore Laxmanpur Beach
  • Photography opportunities at scenic spots
  • Afternoon ferry to Port Blair
  • Free time for shopping or relaxation
  • Overnight stay in Port Blair

Day 5 - Departure from Port Blair

After breakfast and hotel check-out, you have some free time for last-minute shopping or relaxation. Our representative will transfer you to the airport for your onward journey, carrying wonderful memories of your Andaman adventure.

  • Hotel check-out after breakfast
  • Free time for shopping (if time permits)
  • Airport transfer
  • Departure with cherished memories

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to visit Andaman for this package?

The best time to visit Andaman is from October to May when the weather is pleasant with calm seas and clear skies. This period is ideal for water activities, beach visits, and island hopping. Avoid monsoon season (June to September) as ferry services may be disrupted.

Is this package suitable for groups with children?

Yes, absolutely! This 3N/4D package is perfect for groups with children. All activities are group-friendly and safe. The beaches have shallow waters suitable for kids, and our tour coordinators ensure special care for families traveling with children.

Are flight tickets included in the package price?

No, flight tickets to and from Port Blair are not included in the package price. However, we can assist you in booking flights at competitive rates. We recommend booking flights well in advance for better prices and availability.

What type of accommodation is provided in the Standard package?

The Standard package includes comfortable 3-star hotels with clean rooms, modern amenities, and complimentary breakfast. All hotels are centrally located near beaches and major attractions. We can upgrade to Deluxe (4-star) hotels upon request.

Is snorkeling equipment provided, and is it safe for beginners?

Yes, complimentary snorkeling equipment (mask, snorkel, life jacket) is provided at Elephant Beach. Our trained guides ensure complete safety for beginners. Life jackets are mandatory, and shallow water areas are designated for first-timers.

Can I customize this package according to my preferences?

Absolutely! We offer complete customization of itineraries. You can add or remove activities, upgrade hotels, extend your stay, or include additional islands. Contact our team at 8900909900 or 9933202248 to discuss your requirements.

What is the cancellation policy for this package?

Cancellation charges vary based on the time of cancellation: 30+ days before travel - 10% charges, 15-30 days - 25% charges, 7-14 days - 50% charges, less than 7 days - 75% charges. No refund for no-shows. Terms may vary during peak season.

Do I need any special permits to visit Andaman Islands?

Indian citizens do not require any special permits for Port Blair, Havelock, and Neil Island. Foreign nationals need a Restricted Area Permit (RAP) which is issued on arrival at Port Blair airport. All permit charges are included in our package.
