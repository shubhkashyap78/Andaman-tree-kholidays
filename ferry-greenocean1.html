Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the ferry journey take?

The Green Ocean-1 ferry takes approximately 2 hours to travel between Port Blair and Havelock Island. The journey time may vary slightly depending on sea conditions and weather.

What amenities are available on Green Ocean-1?

Green Ocean-1 offers premium amenities including air-conditioned seating, open deck area, complimentary meals and beverages, LCD entertainment screens, clean washrooms, and comfortable seating arrangements.

Can I cancel or reschedule my ferry booking?

Yes, cancellations are allowed up to 24 hours before departure with applicable charges. Rescheduling is subject to seat availability and may incur additional charges. Please contact us at 8900909900 for assistance.

Is food provided on the ferry?

Yes, complimentary snacks and beverages are provided to all passengers on Green Ocean-1. The meals include light refreshments suitable for the journey duration.

What documents do I need to board the ferry?

All passengers must carry a valid government-issued photo ID proof (Aadhaar Card, Passport, Driving License, or Voter ID). Children should carry school ID or birth certificate. Booking confirmation is also required.

How much luggage can I carry?

Each passenger is allowed up to 20kg of luggage. Excess baggage may be charged separately. Hand luggage should be kept with you, while check-in luggage will be stored in the designated area.

Is the ferry safe during monsoon season?

Ferry operations are subject to weather conditions. During rough seas or adverse weather, services may be suspended for passenger safety. We recommend traveling between October to May for the best experience. All ferries are equipped with life jackets and safety equipment.

Can I book tickets on the same day of travel?

While same-day booking is possible subject to availability, we strongly recommend advance booking, especially during peak tourist season (October to March). Advance booking ensures confirmed seats and better pricing.
