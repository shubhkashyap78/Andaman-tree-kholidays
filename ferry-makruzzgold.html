Frequently Asked Questions

Is Makruzz Gold safe?

Yes, Makruzz Gold maintains excellent safety standards with regular maintenance, certified crew, and all required safety equipment including life jackets for every passenger.

What is the luggage allowance?

Each passenger is allowed 20kg of luggage. Excess baggage will be charged separately.

How reliable is Makruzz Gold?

Makruzz Gold has an excellent track record of punctuality and reliability. It's one of the most trusted ferry services in Andaman with thousands of satisfied passengers.

What is the ticket price?

Tickets start from ₹1,200 per person, making it an affordable option without compromising on safety and comfort.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, cancellations are allowed up to 24 hours before departure with applicable charges. Contact us at 8900909900 for cancellation assistance.

Is food available onboard?

Basic refreshments and snacks are available for purchase onboard. You can also bring your own food and beverages.

What documents do I need?

All passengers must carry valid government-issued photo ID proof (Aadhaar, Passport, Driving License, or Voter ID) for verification at the port.
